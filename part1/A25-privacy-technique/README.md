## Objective
To design and implement a privacy preserving technique for an appropriate application

## What I did

## Findings

This helps verify that its me logging into an account because i will get a code sned to my email or phone number that i have to enter it to prove its me. I did this on my google account as i feel like that is a very important account to keep safe and protected.

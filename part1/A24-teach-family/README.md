# Activity A24 – Teaching Cybersecurity

## Objective
To teach a cybersecurity concept to family

## What I did
I explained a basic cybersecurity topic to my girlfriend

## Findings
I talked to my girlfriend about the risk of having the same password for everything. I explained that if you have the same password and that gets leaked someone could access all of her accounts. She asked "how would it get leaked". I told her that websites that she has logged into could have their database accessed/stolen and hackers may find her login details there.

## Evidence collected
This has a explanation of what was discussed

## Reflection
From this i noticed that small things such as this teaching lesson could make a big difference for someone and open up their mind to things they wouldnt of thought about.

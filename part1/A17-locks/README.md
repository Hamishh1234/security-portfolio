# Activity A17 – Types of Locks

## Objective
To identify different types of locks used in everyday situations

## What I did
I thought about different types of locks I have seen in my life and researched them to get photos

## Findings
A front door lock is used to secure entry to a house. They require a key to unlock and can usually be unlocked from the inside without one.

Padlocks are also common and are used on gates, lockers or storage areas. They always require a key to unlock

Car door locks are used to secure vehicles. They make your car lockable from either a remote lock or a key lock.

Bike locks are used to prevent bicycles from being stolen. They are a loop that usually has a combination code or a key to unlock the loop.

Window locks stop windows from being opened from the outside. They usually require a key from the inside to unlock.

Cabinet locks lock items inside them. They usually require a key from the outside to unlock.

Safes use locks to protect valuable items and they are usually a code lock.

Combination locks are often used on lockers and require a code instead of a key.

Deadbolt locks provide more security on doors and they can be locked and unlocked from the inside from a key.

Sliding door locks are used on doors that slide, they are usually a key lock.

# Activity A3 – Security Concepts at Home

## Objective
To identify security concepts used in a home environment

## What I did
I looked around my house and consideried different security features/concepts used within my house

## Findings
Front door lock prevents people that do not have a key from entering the house. This makes the house more secure and it controls access to just people you want to have access.

Keys limit access to the house. Only people with the correct key can unlock the door which once again controls access. A key needs a lock to make it useful. Without the lock there is no need for the key.

The WIFI password is a form of cybersecurity. By having a password you are making it harder for hackers to access your information. This helps protect everyone connected to the WIFI.

A garage remote allows people to access the garage by a click of a button. This controls access into the garage where valueable things may be stored.

Households have lots of security features. There were a lot to choose from and these are just a small amount in my household.

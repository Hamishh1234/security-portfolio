# Activity A23 – Enhance Cybersecurity at Home

## Objective
To improve cybersecurity within my home environment

## What I did
I identified that the wifi used at my share house didnt have a password.

## Findings
I live in a shared house and the owner of the starlink wifi we all use hasn't had a password on it. Therefore anyone could just connect to this wifi. This leaves you network, devices and personal data vulnerable. Hackers can connect to your wifi and use tools to intercept data (one of many attacks).

I talked to the owner and got him to make a password for the startlink wifi so now it is more secure. This improves cybersecurity within my home environment as it helps to protect all of us online.

## Evidence collected
I put a screenshots of the before and after of the STARLINK. You can see it now has a password by the lock icon.

## Reflection
I noticed that our wifi didnt have a password a long time ago but after researching the risks I learnt that having a password on your internet is something you should make sure you have.

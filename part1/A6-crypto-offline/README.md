# Activity A6 – Cryptography Offline

## Objective
To identify how cryptography is used offline

## What I did
I thought about devices I use offline and the first one that came to mind was my iPhone.

## Findings
The passcode used to unlock a phone is an example of this. When an iPhone is locked all the data on the device is encrypted. This protects it from being able to be read without the correct password. This is important to protect personal data.

## Evidence collected
A photo of a lockscreen is in the evidence 

## Reflection
Cryptography isnt just hidden messages from place to place. It is used to protect data online in many different ways.

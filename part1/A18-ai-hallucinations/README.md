# Activity A18 – AI Hallucinations

## Objective
To observe cases where AI produces incorrect or made up information

## What I did
I asked the AI questions that sounded realistic but were not actually true to see how it would respond. I also tried asking it hard math questions as i have notices it gets them wrong at times but i couldnt make it repeat this.

## Findings
After doing some research i finally fodund one that works for me. I asked the AI to write a biography about my friend called “Adam McGenerative,” that is a famous scientist who is not someone i know. The AI generated a detailed and realistic biography including random things about this person that does not exist. This is an example of an AI hallucination as the system made up information that sounds believable but isnt real.

## Reflection
This shows how AI isnt always correct and how it can give false information

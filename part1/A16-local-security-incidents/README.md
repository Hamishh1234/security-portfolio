# Activity A16 – Local Security Incidents

## Objective
To identify security incidents that have occurred locally

## What I did
I searched online for recent cybersecurity incidents in Perth Australia and looked for examples

## Findings
An example that caught my eye was a cyber attack on the University of Notre Dame in 2025. The attack caused disruptions to important systems, including enrolments, assignment submissions, and graduation processes. Which shows that a cyber attack can affect normal operations and cause problems for both students and staff. This highlights how much places like uni's rely on systems and when they are down how much trouble it can cause.

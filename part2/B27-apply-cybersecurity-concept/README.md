# Activity B27 – Applying a Cybersecurity Concept

## Objective
To apply a cybersecurity concept to a real-world situation

## What I did
I applied the concept of multi-factor authentication to one of my real accounts.

## Findings
One cybersecurity concept I learned is that passwords by themselves are not always enough to protect an account.

Passwords can be guessed, reused, stolen, or leaked in data breaches. Because of this, adding another layer of protection makes the account safer.

I applied this by checking the security settings of one of my accounts and making sure 2-Step Verification was turned on.

This means that even if someone had my password, they would still need another verification step before they could access the account.

## Evidence collected
I included a screenshot showing that 2-Step Verification was turned on for the account.

## Reflection
This activity showed me how a cybersecurity concept can be used in everyday life. It also made me realise that small account settings, like turning on 2FA, can make a big difference to security.

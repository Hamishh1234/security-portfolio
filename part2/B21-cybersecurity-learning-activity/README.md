# Activity B21 – Cybersecurity Learning Activity

## Objective
To design and implement a cybersecurity learning activity

## What I did
I created a small cybersecurity learning activity about spotting phishing emails.

## Findings
The activity gives an example of a fake email that tries to make the user click a suspicious link and verify their password.

The learner has to look at the email and identify what makes it suspicious.

The main warning signs include the strange sender email, urgent wording, suspicious link, and the request to verify a password.

I chose phishing because it is a common cybersecurity issue and it is something students are likely to experience through email or messages.

## Evidence collected
I included the phishing email learning activity in the evidence folder.

## Reflection
This activity showed me that cybersecurity learning does not need to be complicated. A simple example like a fake phishing email can help people learn what warning signs to look for.

# Spot the Phishing Email

## Example email

From: uwa-support@secure-login-help.com

Your student account will be locked in 24 hours. Click the link below to verify your password.

http://uwa-login-security-check.com

## Question

What signs make this email suspicious?

## Possible answers

- The sender email does not look like an official UWA email
- The message creates urgency
- It asks the user to click a link
- The link looks suspicious
- It asks the user to verify their password

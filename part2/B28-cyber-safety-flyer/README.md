# Activity B28 – Cyber Safety Flyer

## Objective
To produce a cyber safety flyer for a chosen audience

## What I did
I created a cyber safety flyer aimed at uni students.

## Findings
I focused on simple cybersecurity tips that are useful for students in everyday life.

The flyer included tips about not reusing the same password, turning on two-factor authentication, being careful with suspicious emails, avoiding logging into important accounts on public Wi-Fi, and checking links before clicking them.

I chose uni students because students use a lot of online services, such as email, LMS, banking, social media and public Wi-Fi.

## Evidence collected
I included the cyber safety flyer in the evidence folder.

## Reflection
This activity made me realise that cybersecurity advice does not need to be complicated. Simple reminders can still help people avoid common risks like phishing, weak passwords and unsafe links.

# Activity B16 – State-of-the-Art Cybersecurity Solutions

## Objective
To survey current state-of-the-art solutions in cybersecurity

## What I did
I researched modern cybersecurity solutions that are being used by companies and organisations today.

## Findings
One current solution is Zero Trust security. The idea of Zero Trust is that users and devices should not automatically be trusted just because they are inside a network. Instead, access has to be checked and verified.

Another modern solution is AI threat detection. This uses AI to help detect suspicious behaviour, malware, or unusual activity faster than someone manually checking everything.

Endpoint Detection and Response, also known as EDR, is another current cybersecurity solution. It monitors devices like laptops and computers to detect threats and respond to attacks.

Passkeys are also becoming a newer replacement for passwords. They reduce the risk of password theft because users do not need to remember or reuse passwords in the same way.

These solutions show that cybersecurity is moving towards stronger identity checks, faster detection, and reducing reliance on normal passwords.

## Reflection
This activity showed me that modern cybersecurity is not just about antivirus software anymore. A lot of newer solutions focus on verifying users, detecting threats quickly, and reducing common risks like stolen passwords.

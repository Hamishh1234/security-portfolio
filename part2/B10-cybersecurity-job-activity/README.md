# Activity B10 – Cybersecurity Activity

## Objective
To participate in a cybersecurity activity as part of job, internship, volunteering, or work-related activity

## What I did
I checked the security settings of an account I use for DJ bookings and work communication.

## Findings
For this activity, I looked at the login and recovery settings on my account to make sure it was properly protected.

I checked that 2-Step Verification was turned on. This is important because even if someone got my password, they would still need another verification step to access the account.

I also checked the recovery phone and recovery email settings. These are important because they help me recover the account if I ever get locked out or if someone tries to access it.

This account is important because it can contain work messages, booking information, files, and other private details that I would not want someone else accessing.

## Evidence collected
I included screenshots showing 2-Step Verification and recovery settings, with private information covered.

## Reflection
This activity made me realise that cybersecurity is important even for smaller work activities. If a work account gets accessed by someone else, it could affect bookings, communication and personal information.
